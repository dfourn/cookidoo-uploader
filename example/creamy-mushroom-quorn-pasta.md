# Creamy Mushroom & Quorn Pasta (TM6)

**Serves 2 · ~25 min**

---

## Ingredients
- 300g chestnut mushrooms
- ~280g Quorn chicken-style pieces (1 bag, from frozen is fine) *(optional)*
- 1 onion, halved
- 2 garlic cloves
- 35g Parmesan, in chunks
- 20g butter
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 100ml dry white wine *(optional — or swap for 100ml stock)*
- 150ml double cream
- A few sprigs fresh thyme (or 1 tsp dried)
- 200g pasta (tagliatelle, penne, or rigatoni)
- Salt & black pepper
- Fresh parsley + reserved Parmesan, to serve

---

## Method

### Step 1 — Grate Parmesan
Add 30g Parmesan to TM bowl (save ~5g for serving).
**Grate: 10 sec / Speed 10**

Set aside.

### Step 2 — Chop mushrooms
Add 300g mushrooms (halve any big ones).
**Chop: 3 sec / Speed 4**

Set aside.

### Step 3 — Chop onion + garlic
Add 1 onion (halved) + 2 garlic cloves.
**Chop: 5 sec / Speed 7**

Scrape down.

### Step 4 — Sauté the base
Add 20g butter + 1 tbsp olive oil.
**Cook: 3 min / 120°C / Speed 1**

### Step 5 — Cook mushrooms (+ Quorn) + wine
Add to TM bowl:
- 300g chopped mushrooms (reserved)
- 100ml white wine (or stock)
- A few sprigs fresh thyme (or 1 tsp dried)
- 280g Quorn pieces *(if using)*

**Cook: 10 min / 100°C / Reverse / Speed Soft Stir** — *measuring cup off* to let the liquid reduce.

*(Put pasta on now: 200g in salted boiling water, cook to al dente, and save a mugful of pasta water before draining.)*

### Step 6 — Finish the sauce
Add 150ml double cream + 30g grated Parmesan.
**Cook: 2 min / 90°C / Reverse / Speed Soft Stir**

Season with salt & pepper. Loosen with a splash of pasta water if too thick.

### Step 7 — Serve
Divide the drained 200g pasta between serving bowls or containers, then spoon the sauce over the top (no need to mix it through the TM bowl). Finish with the reserved 5g Parmesan, fresh parsley, and black pepper. Loosen with a splash of pasta water if needed.

---

## Notes
- **If using Quorn:** it's pre-cooked, so it only needs heating through — Reverse / Soft Stir keeps the pieces intact and stops them going spongy. It adds no fat/juices, so season well and lean on the pasta water for a silky sauce.
- **Stock tip:** for a savory boost (especially with Quorn, which brings no meaty juices), either add 100ml chicken/veg stock at step 5 (cup off so it still reduces), or crumble in ½ a stock cube / 1 tsp stock paste for flavor without thinning the sauce.
- **Cream cheese swap:** use 100g full-fat cream cheese in place of the double cream, and loosen with pasta water to a silky consistency.
