# Chicken Tikka Masala (TM6)

**Serves 3**

> Scaled to 3 from the 2-portion version, keeping whole tins (no half-tins) so it all still fits the TM6 bowl. The chicken, spices and onions are scaled up; the tomato and coconut milk stay at one tin each, so the sauce is on the thicker side — loosen with a splash of water if you like.

---

## Ingredients

### Marinade
- 650–950g chicken breast fillets, cut into bite-size chunks (whatever standard pack is nearest)
- 4 tbsp natural yogurt
- 1.5 tsp garam masala
- 1.5 tsp tikka curry powder
- Salt & pepper

### Sauce
- 3 onions, halved
- 4 garlic cloves
- 1 large thumb-sized piece fresh ginger, peeled
- 1.5 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tin chopped tomatoes (400g)
- 1 tin coconut milk (400ml)
- 1.5 tbsp tomato puree
- 1 tbsp tikka curry powder (3 tsp)
- 1.5 tsp garam masala
- 1.5 tsp ground cumin
- 1.5 tsp ground coriander
- 3/4 tsp turmeric
- 1 tbsp natural yogurt (to finish — from the same pot as the marinade)

### To serve
- Basmati rice
- Fresh coriander, chopped
- Squeeze of lemon

---

## Method

### Marinate the chicken (30 min before, or while prepping)
Mix together:
- 650–950g chicken breast, cut into bite-size chunks
- 4 tbsp natural yogurt
- 1.5 tsp garam masala
- 1.5 tsp tikka curry powder
- Pinch of salt

Set aside.

---

### Step 1 — Sauce base
Add to TM bowl:
- 3 onions (halved)
- 4 garlic cloves
- 1 large thumb-sized piece fresh ginger, peeled

**Chop: 5 sec / Speed 7**

Scrape down the lid and sides.

### Step 2 — Saute
Add 1.5 tbsp olive oil.
**Cook: 5 min / 120°C / Speed 1**

### Step 3 — Spices
Add to TM bowl:
- 1 tbsp tikka curry powder
- 1.5 tsp garam masala
- 1.5 tsp ground cumin
- 1.5 tsp ground coriander
- 3/4 tsp turmeric
- 1.5 tbsp tomato puree

**Cook: 2 min / 120°C / Speed 1**

### Step 4 — Simmer the sauce
Add to TM bowl:
- 1 tin chopped tomatoes (400g)
- 1 tin coconut milk (400ml)

**Cook: 15 min / 100°C / Reverse / Speed 1**

### Step 5 — Blend (optional, for smoother sauce)
**Blend: 10 sec / Speed 5–7**

Skip if you prefer a chunkier sauce.

### Step 6 — Cook the chicken
Add marinated chicken pieces to the sauce.
**Cook: 22 min / 100°C / Reverse / Speed Soft Stir**

### Step 7 — Finish
Stir in 1 tbsp natural yogurt (the sauce should be off the boil so it doesn't split). Taste and adjust salt. The coconut milk already gives plenty of richness, so this just adds a final tang.

---

## Serve
Over basmati rice, topped with fresh coriander and a squeeze of lemon.

**Rice (Yum Asia Panda):** Rinse well until water runs clear. Add to the inner bowl and fill to the **LONG GRAIN** water line for the number of cups you're cooking (use the provided measuring cup) — ~1 cup per person, so ~3 cups for 3. Select the **LONG GRAIN** program. Start it around Step 4 so it's ready when the chicken is done. When it switches to Keep Warm, fluff immediately with the spatula.

---

## Notes
- A full tin of drained chickpeas adds substance, but at this scale it pushes the bowl past the 2.2L max — so either leave them out, or swap ~200g of the chicken for them.
